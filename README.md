Core Features: Rewriting the Physics of Cognition
🌌 4D Cognitive Topology
Break free from the rigid "2D tree folders" and chaotic "flat spiderwebs" of traditional note-taking apps. Wu Universe uses celestial orbits as a metaphor, projecting your knowledge into a true 3D starry sky while introducing the dimension of time. You are no longer building a warehouse for notes, but a multidimensional, navigable memory palace.

🧲 Semantic Gravity Engine
Say goodbye to manual tagging and rigid, forced links. Our underlying semantic algorithms automatically capture hidden correlations you haven't even realized yet. Knowledge nodes are pulled together by semantic gravity, spontaneously forming massive, brilliant cognitive nebulas. No more endless wandering—let the answers gravitate toward you.

⏳ Knowledge Survival & Collapse Mechanism
Knowledge has a lifecycle. We've pioneered a "gamified survival mechanism"—knowledge nodes that are neglected will not only dim and shatter, but their gravitational links will slowly detach and drift away. Conversely, when reviewed, they ignite with the brilliance of a supernova and return to stable high-orbits. The forgetting curve is visualized as the birth and death of the cosmos.

🪐 Fractal Architecture & Space Folding
The fractal beauty of nature is reborn here. From a macro perspective, you witness the tides of nebulas; double-click to warp into a specific planet, and you will find a micro-stellar system with precise planetary orbits. A world in a grain of sand—hierarchical, clear, and never tangled.

🔒 Human-AI Co-Creation & The "God Lock"
In this co-created universe, the AI's gravity engine discovers hidden connections and drives natural evolution. However, you hold the absolute "God Lock." The orbits you pin down will never shift, even if the universe ends. You provide the unshakeable foundation; gravity handles the cosmic emergence.
